About Kidney Tumor Detection

This project, Kidney Tumor Detection in CT Scans: Combining 3D CNN and Transformer Networks aims to revolutionize early and accurate diagnosis of renal conditions using state of the art deep learning.

We leverage cutting edge deep learning architectures, specifically ConvNeXt for robust spatial feature extraction and Vision Transformers (ViT) for capturing long-range dependencies and contextual information within CT scans.

Our goal is to provide medical professionals with a powerful, reliable, and efficient tool to assist in the detection process, ultimately leading to earlier interventions and improved patient outcomes. The system classifies kidney CT images into four categories: Normal, Cyst, Tumor, and Stone.

Our Approach

ConvNeXt Backbone

Depthwise separable convolutions for efficient spatial feature learning from CT data.

Vision Transformer

Self-attention mechanism to understand complex global relationships within each scan.

Data Security

Ensuring the highest standards of patient data privacy and security at every step.

High Accuracy

Continuously improving our hybrid model for superior detection rates across all classes.
